Layover Playbooks

What to do with each pause

Each plan keeps a conservative return buffer. When sleep and immigration compete, sleep usually wins.

DTW - Aug 16 night

Turn the 13h 19m stop into real sleep.

Best move: book the Westin Detroit Metropolitan Airport or another very close airport hotel. Your Delta flights use McNamara, and this layover is long enough for a full overnight reset without going into Detroit.

  1. 11:21pLand at DTW and move slowly. No city run.
  2. 12:10aCheck in, shower, set two alarms.
  3. 8:45aWake, breakfast, refill bottle, repack overnight kit.
  4. 10:00aBack toward security and the Delta departure area.
  5. 11:30aGate check for DL 159 to Incheon.

Carry what you need for the night. If checked bags are through-tagged to Bali, assume you will not see them in Detroit.

ICN - Aug 18 evening

Keep the 1h 35m connection airside.

Treat this as a controlled transfer: restroom, water, a short walk, gate. Delta and Korean Air are listed at Terminal 2, but gate changes can still eat the whole buffer.

  1. 4:25pArrive from DTW, follow transfer signs.
  2. 4:55pClear any transfer screening.
  3. 5:15pGrab water or a compact snack near the gate.
  4. 5:25pBe in the DPS gate area.
  5. 6:00pDepart for Bali.

Do not attempt immigration, a lounge hunt, or shopping across terminals on this one.

ICN - Aug 25 daytime

Use the 9h 25m stop, but keep it airport-led.

Best move: reserve an official Incheon Transit Tour if your entry permission is clear. The desk requires passport plus both boarding passes, and you need to be there 30 minutes before the tour. If timing or entry paperwork is fuzzy, stay inside ICN.

  1. 9:25aLand, then decide based on immigration and energy.
  2. 10:45aLatest comfortable time to be landside for any tour/city option.
  3. 11:15aTransit tour, Seoul Station lunch, or Hongdae walk.
  4. 2:45pStart the return to ICN. No heroic last stops.
  5. 4:00pBack at Terminal 2 for exit process and DL 26.

AREX shows the airport-to-Seoul rail trip as roughly 67 minutes from the airport flow, so a self-guided city visit needs a strict turn-back time.

ATL - Aug 25 night

Make the 10h 10m stop a protected sleep block.

Best move: skip downtown and book one of the Gateway/SkyTrain airport hotels. You will be arriving internationally, so budget time for U.S. entry, baggage handling, and getting landside before sleep.

  1. 7:50pArrive ATL from Incheon.
  2. 9:15pCustoms, bags, recheck, then hotel transfer.
  3. 10:00pSleep near the airport.
  4. 3:45aLeave hotel for terminal and TSA.
  5. 5:20aAt the LGA gate with coffee and no sprinting.

ATL says the SkyTrain runs 24/7 and connects to several Gateway hotels. That is the calmest overnight option.

Arrival Admin

Documents and forms to handle before Bali

Check these in the week before departure. Requirements can change, and the final authority is the government or airport source linked below.

Indonesia entry

  • Passport valid at least six months beyond arrival.
  • Two blank visa pages for the entry stamp.
  • Visa or e-VOA/VOA for tourism, plus onward or return ticket.
  • Keep a copy of passport and visa/e-VOA confirmation offline.

Bali-specific

  • Pay or prepare the Bali tourist levy: IDR 150,000.
  • Complete any required customs or arrival declaration before landing if available.
  • Use an official airport taxi, hotel transfer, or trusted ride app after midnight.
  • Keep the first 24 hours light: sleep, SIM/eSIM, cash, sunset.

Connection habits

  • Screenshot boarding passes and hotel confirmations.
  • Pack one overnight kit in your personal item.
  • Set phone clock widgets for Detroit, Incheon, Bali, Atlanta, and New York.
  • Re-check Delta/Korean Air gates on every airport Wi-Fi login.
